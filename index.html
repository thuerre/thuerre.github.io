About Me

Hello! I'm Timothé, 4th year (post-graduate) student in Game Design at Supinfogame Rubika, France.

I've been making games for 5 years, mostly video games using Unity & UE4.


Engaging myself into creating
fair & immersives experiences is my leitmotiv, and I hope to be able to do it for the many years to come.

Sometimes, I'm also a chill gentleman who loves to hang out, tries to practice sports regularly and has many other interests!
