About Me

I am a 3rd year student in Game Design at Supinfogame Rubika, France.

I've been making games for 3 years, mostly video games using Unity & UE4.

I'd like to offer the players a way to meet, evade and have fun together.

Engaging myself into creating fun experiences is my leitmotiv, and I hope to be able to do it for the many years to come.

Among everything, I love to design systems, to analyse & balance content and to make prototypes.
